I'm currently a Ph.D. student at DAMSLab (data management for data science laboratory) at the Graz University of Technology (TU Graz) under the supervision of Prof. Matthias Boehm. Before joining TU Graz, I was a Research Assistant in Database Systems Research Group at the Faculty of Computer Science of the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano. Previously, I was a Research Staff Member in Distributed Systems Lab at Computer Engineering Department, Iran University of Science and Technology.

Interests: The purpose of my research is to study data analytics: how to analyze, store, retrieve, and manipulate large and heterogeneous data sets. My research focuses on systems-oriented problems that arise when developing software to manage and process large and diverse data sets, especially machine learning systems.
